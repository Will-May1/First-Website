This is The Onceler

"Unless someone like you cares a whole awful lot,
nothing is going to get better.
It's not." - The Onceler, 2012

The Onceler was a greedy industrialist who cut down
all of the multicolored Truffula Trees to make
a multipurpose garment known as a Thneed,
a "Fine-Something-That-All-People-Need."
